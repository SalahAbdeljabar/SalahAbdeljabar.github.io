Direct mentor for an undergraduate intern in the [Communication Theory Lab](https://ctl.kaust.edu.sa/) at KAUST as part of the [Microelectronics Winter Camp (MEWC 2026)](https://cemse.kaust.edu.sa/research/microelectronics-winter-camp).
